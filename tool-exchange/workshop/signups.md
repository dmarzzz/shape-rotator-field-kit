## How to add yourself

Replace one of the placeholder lines (or append a new one) in the
session you want. Keep it one line per person:

```
- [x] @yourhandle — bringing: <tool>, looking for: <kind of feedback or pairing>
```

The `[x]` is cosmetic; treat it as "signed up."
